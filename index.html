Privacy Policy for Racket Empire

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Racket Empire ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

Racket Empire does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data directly. We do not require user accounts, registration, or login. There is no cloud storage or server-side data collection.

Local Game Data

All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services that may collect certain data:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data including:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • IP address (for approximate location to serve relevant ads)
  • Ad interaction data

Google AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use their services, visit: Google's Partner Sites Policy.

Google Play In-App Billing

The Game offers optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play's billing system. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information, credit card details, or billing data.

Google Play's billing practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Children's Privacy

Racket Empire is not directed at children under the age of 13. The Game contains simulated crime themes and is intended for a mature audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data retention on our servers. Local game data remains on your device until you uninstall the Game or clear its data.

Your Rights

You can control ad personalization through your device's settings:

  • On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

You can delete all local game data by uninstalling the Game or clearing app data through your device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
